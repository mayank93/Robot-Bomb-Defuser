The game is to diffuse the bomb planted by enemies at different places.The bomb can be diffused by collecting a particular number of codes which are located at different places.Each of these codes contains vital information on how to defuse the bomb.You have to collect all these codes with the help of a robot in order to diffuse the bombs.Be careful from the enemeis , if you come closer to them you are dead .If you touch the bomb before collecting codes the bomb get explode.Bomb may be hidden partially beside the codes.
